## Creating a team

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click **New Team** (Pro plan required)
3. Enter a team name
4. You'll be the team owner

## Inviting members

1. Open your team settings
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Choose a role: Admin, Editor, or Viewer
5. They'll receive an email invitation

## Roles

| Role | Can do |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | Everything — billing, delete team, manage all members |
| **Admin** | Manage members, edit settings, create projects |
| **Editor** | Create and edit projects |
| **Viewer** | View projects (read-only) |

## Switching between teams

If you belong to multiple teams, use the workspace switcher in the dashboard to switch between them.
