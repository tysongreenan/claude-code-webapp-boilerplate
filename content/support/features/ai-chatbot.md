## What is the AI chatbot?

The chat bubble in the bottom-right corner is an AI assistant that knows about our product. Ask it anything — it searches our knowledge base and gives you an answer.

## Tips for getting good answers

- **Be specific** — "How do I invite a team member?" works better than "teams"
- **Ask follow-ups** — the chat remembers the conversation context
- **Try different phrasing** — if the first answer isn't helpful, rephrase your question

## What it knows about

The chatbot is trained on:
- All support articles (this help center)
- Product documentation
- Blog posts
- FAQ content

## What it can't do

- Access your account data
- Make changes to your account
- Process payments or refunds
- Access real-time information (it uses pre-indexed content)

If the chatbot can't help, contact our support team directly.
