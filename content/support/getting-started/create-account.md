## Creating your account

1. Click **Get Started** on the homepage or go to the sign-up page
2. Choose to sign up with **email and password** or **Google**
3. If using email, enter your name, email address, and a password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Verify your email address if prompted
5. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## Signing in with Google

Click "Sign up with Google" and authorize the app. Your name and profile photo will be imported automatically. No password needed.

## What happens after sign-up

- A personal workspace is created for you automatically
- You start on the **Free plan**
- You can upgrade to Pro from your dashboard at any time
