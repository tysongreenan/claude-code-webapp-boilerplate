## Your dashboard

After signing in, you land on your dashboard. Here's what you'll find:

### Welcome card
Shows your name and current plan (Free or Pro).

### Projects
Your projects appear here. Click **New Project** to create one. Projects update in real-time — if a teammate creates one, it appears instantly.

### Upgrade card
If you're on the Free plan, this shows you what's available on Pro.

## Navigation

- **Dashboard** — your main workspace
- **Admin** — site management (admin users only)
- **Blog** — public articles and guides
- **Sign Out** — via the user menu in the top right
