## Free plan

The Free plan includes:
- 1 workspace
- Basic project creation
- Access to the AI chatbot
- Blog access

## Pro plan

The Pro plan includes everything in Free, plus:
- Unlimited projects
- Team workspaces with roles
- Priority support
- Advanced analytics
- All future features

## How to upgrade

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click **View Plans** or the upgrade card
3. Choose monthly or annual billing
4. Complete checkout via Stripe
5. Your plan upgrades instantly

## Billing management

After upgrading, manage your subscription:
- Change payment method
- Switch between monthly and annual
- Cancel your subscription
- View invoices

Go to **Dashboard** → click your profile → **Manage Billing** to access the Stripe customer portal.
