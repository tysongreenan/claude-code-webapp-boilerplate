## Canceling your subscription

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Manage Billing**
2. Click **Cancel subscription** in the Stripe portal
3. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period
4. After that, you'll be moved to the Free plan

## Requesting a refund

If you're not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of purchase for a full refund. After 14 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

## What happens when you cancel

- Your projects and data are preserved
- You keep Free plan access
- Team features are disabled
- You can re-subscribe at any time and your data will be there
