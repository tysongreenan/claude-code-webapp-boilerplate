## I can't sign in

- **Check your email** — make sure you're using the same email you signed up with
- **Google account** — if you signed up with Google, click "Sign in with Google" (not email/password)
- **Password reset** — click "Forgot password?" on the sign-in page
- **Clear cookies** — try clearing your browser cookies and signing in again

## The page won't load

- **Check your internet** — try refreshing or opening another website
- **Clear cache** — hard refresh with Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
- **Try incognito** — open the site in an incognito/private window
- **Status page** — check if the service is experiencing downtime

## My changes aren't saving

- **Check your connection** — the app needs an internet connection to save
- **Refresh** — try refreshing the page
- **Check your role** — Viewers can't make changes (need Editor or above)

## Payment failed

- **Check your card** — make sure the card hasn't expired and has sufficient funds
- **Try another card** — go to Manage Billing → update payment method
- **Contact your bank** — some banks block online transactions by default

## I was charged but my plan didn't upgrade

This usually resolves within a few minutes. If it doesn't:
1. Sign out and sign back in
2. Check your email for a payment confirmation
3. Contact support with your payment receipt
