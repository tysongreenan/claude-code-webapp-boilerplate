## Before contacting support

1. **Search this help center** — your answer may already be here
2. **Ask the AI chatbot** — click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner
3. **Check common issues** — see our troubleshooting guide

## How to reach us

- **AI Chatbot** — available 24/7 in the bottom-right corner
- **Email** — support@yourdomain.com (replace with your actual email)
- **Response time** — we aim to respond within 24 hours on business days

## What to include in your message

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:
- Your account email address
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead (include any error messages)
- Screenshots if possible
